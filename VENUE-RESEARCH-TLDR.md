## Cost Reality Check

### East Valley Wedding (100-120 guests)
**Total Budget: $25,000-$32,000**

Breakdown:
- Venue + Food: $10K
- Photos: $4K
- DJ: $1.5K  
- Flowers: $3.5K
- Everything else: $10K

### Sedona Wedding (100 guests)
**Total Budget: $55,000-$70,000**

Why so much more:
- Venue rental: $12K (vs $4-7K)
- More vendors to coordinate
- Higher vendor costs
- Travel costs
- Destination logistics

**Cost Difference: $32K more for Sedona**

## Quick Decision Matrix

**Choose East Valley IF:**
- Budget matters ($25-32K total vs $55-70K)
- Most guests live in Phoenix area
- Want convenience and all-inclusive
- Prefer climate-controlled indoor space
- Want less planning stress

**Choose Sedona IF:**
- Scenery is top priority
- Budget can handle $55-70K total
- Smaller guest count (under 100)
- Guests traveling anyway
- Want destination wedding feel
- Photography is extremely important
