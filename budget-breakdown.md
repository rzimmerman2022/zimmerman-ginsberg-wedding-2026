## Money-Saving Tips for Arizona October Wedding

### Venue & Catering
- Consider all-inclusive packages
- Weekday or Sunday wedding for lower rates
- Buffet vs. plated service
- Limited bar vs. open bar
- Skip champagne toast (use signature cocktail)

### Photography
- Book engagement photos separately or skip
- Limit hours of coverage
- Digital-only package (skip physical album initially)

### Flowers & Decor
- Use seasonal Arizona flowers
- Greenery-heavy arrangements
- Repurpose ceremony flowers at reception
- DIY some decor elements
- Rent decor items vs. purchasing

### Attire
- Sample sale dresses
- Rent tuxedos
- Buy bridesmaid dresses off-the-rack
- Minimal alterations

### Stationery
- Digital save-the-dates
- Online invitations (eco-friendly option)
- DIY programs and menus
- Skip favors (most are forgotten)

### General
- Smaller guest list = biggest savings
- Off-season honeymoon destination
- Friday or Sunday wedding
- Brunch or lunch reception vs. dinner

## Notes

- Arizona wedding costs vary significantly by location (Phoenix/Scottsdale typically higher than Tucson)
- October is peak season - expect less flexibility on pricing
- Always ask vendors what's included and what costs extra
- Get all agreements in writing
- Factor in sales tax (varies by county: 5.6% to 11.2%)
- Budget 5-10% contingency for unexpected costs
