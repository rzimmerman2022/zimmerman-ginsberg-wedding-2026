# Wedding Planning Master Timeline

**Target Wedding Date:** October 2026 (specific date TBD)  
**Location:** Arizona

---

## Timeline Overview

This timeline works backwards from an October 2026 wedding date. Adjust specific months once exact date is confirmed.

---

## 18-24 Months Before Wedding (Apr-Oct 2025)

**Planning Phase: Foundation**

- [ ] Announce engagement to family and friends
- [ ] Determine wedding budget and who's contributing
- [ ] Draft initial guest list (rough headcount)
- [ ] Discuss wedding vision, style, and priorities
- [ ] Research wedding insurance options
- [ ] Start Pinterest/inspiration boards
- [ ] Consider hiring wedding planner (optional)

---

## 12-18 Months Before (Apr-Oct 2025) **‚Üê CURRENT PHASE**

**Planning Phase: Major Decisions**

### Venue & Date
- [ ] Research Arizona venues (Phoenix, Scottsdale, Sedona, Tucson, Flagstaff)
- [ ] Schedule venue tours
- [ ] Compare venue packages and pricing
- [ ] Lock in specific October 2026 date
- [ ] Sign venue contract and pay deposit
- [ ] Confirm backup/inclement weather plan

### Wedding Party
- [ ] Choose wedding party members
- [ ] Ask bridesmaids/groomsmen to participate

### Initial Vendor Research
- [ ] Start photographer research
- [ ] Research caterers (if not venue-provided)
- [ ] Research florists
- [ ] Research entertainment options

### Guest List
- [ ] Finalize guest list headcount
- [ ] Collect addresses for save-the-dates

---

## 9-11 Months Before (Nov 2025-Jan 2026)

**Planning Phase: Core Vendors**

### Major Vendors
- [ ] Book photographer/videographer
- [ ] Book caterer and finalize menu selections
- [ ] Book florist and discuss floral vision
- [ ] Book band/DJ for reception
- [ ] Book ceremony musicians (if different from reception)

### Communication
- [ ] Design and order save-the-dates
- [ ] Create wedding website
- [ ] Send save-the-dates (especially for out-of-town guests)

### Attire
- [ ] Start shopping for wedding dress/suit
- [ ] Research attire for wedding party

---

## 6-8 Months Before (Feb-Apr 2026)

**Planning Phase: Details & Reservations**

### Ceremony
- [ ] Book officiant
- [ ] Plan ceremony structure and readings
- [ ] Select ceremony music

### Guest Accommodations
- [ ] Reserve hotel room blocks for guests
- [ ] Add hotel information to wedding website

### Invitations
- [ ] Order wedding invitations and response cards
- [ ] Order thank-you cards

### Additional Services
- [ ] Book hair stylist and makeup artist
- [ ] Book transportation (limo, shuttle, etc.)

### Events
- [ ] Plan rehearsal dinner venue and details
- [ ] Consider welcome party for out-of-town guests
- [ ] Plan post-wedding brunch (optional)

### Honeymoon
- [ ] Research and book honeymoon
- [ ] Ensure passports are current (if international)

---

## 4-6 Months Before (Apr-Jun 2026)

**Planning Phase: Finalization**

### Invitations
- [ ] Mail wedding invitations (6-8 weeks before)
- [ ] Set up RSVP tracking system
- [ ] Update wedding website with final details

### Attire
- [ ] Order wedding dress/suit if not already done
- [ ] Order wedding party attire
- [ ] Order rings if not already done

### Registry
- [ ] Set up wedding registry
- [ ] Add registry info to wedding website

### Rentals & Decor
- [ ] Order rental items (chairs, linens, etc.)
- [ ] Finalize reception decor plan
- [ ] Order wedding favors (if doing them)

### Legal
- [ ] Research marriage license requirements for Arizona
- [ ] Decide on name change plans (if applicable)

---

## 2-3 Months Before (Jul-Aug 2026)

**Planning Phase: Confirmation**

### Vendor Coordination
- [ ] Confirm details with all vendors
- [ ] Provide final guest count estimates
- [ ] Schedule final venue walkthrough
- [ ] Create detailed day-of timeline for vendors

### Attire
- [ ] Wedding dress/suit alterations and fittings
- [ ] Break in wedding shoes
- [ ] Purchase wedding accessories

### Guest Management
- [ ] Follow up on missing RSVPs
- [ ] Begin seating chart planning
- [ ] Coordinate transportation for wedding party

### Legal & Administrative
- [ ] Apply for marriage license (check Arizona timing requirements)
- [ ] Review name change paperwork if applicable
- [ ] Confirm vendors have certificates of insurance

---

## 1 Month Before (Sep 2026)

**Planning Phase: Final Countdown**

### Finalization
- [ ] Provide final guest count to caterer
- [ ] Finalize and submit seating chart
- [ ] Confirm final timeline with all vendors
- [ ] Final dress/suit fitting

### Payments
- [ ] Make final payments to all vendors
- [ ] Prepare vendor tip envelopes
- [ ] Designate someone to distribute tips on wedding day

### Rehearsal
- [ ] Schedule rehearsal (typically night before or 2 days before)
- [ ] Confirm rehearsal dinner details and attendees

### Communication
- [ ] Send timeline to wedding party
- [ ] Brief wedding party on responsibilities
- [ ] Prepare wedding day emergency kit

### Personal
- [ ] Get marriage license from county clerk
- [ ] Write vows (if doing personal vows)
- [ ] Prepare toasts/speeches
- [ ] Get final haircut/grooming appointments

---

## 1 Week Before

**Planning Phase: Last-Minute Details**

- [ ] Confirm all vendor arrival times
- [ ] Pack for wedding night and honeymoon
- [ ] Break in wedding shoes one final time
- [ ] Drop off items at venue (if allowed)
- [ ] Give rings to best man/maid of honor
- [ ] Rehearsal and rehearsal dinner
- [ ] Get good sleep leading up to the day

---

## Wedding Day

**THE BIG DAY!**

- [ ] Eat breakfast
- [ ] Follow hair/makeup timeline
- [ ] First look photos (if doing)
- [ ] Ceremony
- [ ] Cocktail hour
- [ ] Reception
- [ ] Grand exit
- [ ] Enjoy being married!

---

## After the Wedding

**Planning Phase: Post-Wedding**

### Immediate (1 Week After)
- [ ] Return rental items
- [ ] Preserve wedding dress (if desired)
- [ ] Return wedding attire rentals

### Short-term (1 Month After)
- [ ] Write and send thank-you notes
- [ ] Change name on documents (if applicable)
- [ ] Review and select photos from photographer
- [ ] Leave vendor reviews

### Long-term (2-3 Months After)
- [ ] Order wedding album
- [ ] Frame favorite photos
- [ ] Organize and back up all wedding photos/videos
- [ ] Submit wedding announcement to newspapers (if desired)

---

## Notes

- This timeline assumes booking vendors 12-18 months out for an October wedding in Arizona
- October is peak season in Arizona - venues and top vendors book early
- Adjust timeline based on specific venue requirements and vendor availability
- Build buffer time into schedule for unexpected delays
