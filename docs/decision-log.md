# Wedding Decision Log

This document tracks all major decisions made during wedding planning. Use this to reference why certain choices were made and to maintain consistency throughout the planning process.

---

## How to Use This Log

For each decision, record:
- **Date:** When the decision was made
- **Decision:** What was decided
- **Options Considered:** What alternatives were evaluated
- **Reasoning:** Why this option was chosen
- **Impact:** Budget, timeline, or other considerations
- **Status:** Finalized, Pending Confirmation, or Under Review
