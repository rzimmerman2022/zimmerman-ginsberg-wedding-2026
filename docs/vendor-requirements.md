# Vendor Requirements & Comparison Guide

This document outlines what to look for when researching and comparing wedding vendors.

---

## Venue

### Must-Have Requirements
- [ ] Available for October 2026
- [ ] Capacity for ____ guests (determine final count)
- [ ] Indoor/outdoor options
- [ ] Backup plan for weather
- [ ] Adequate parking or valet service
- [ ] Accessible facilities (ADA compliant)
- [ ] Getting-ready spaces for wedding party

### Questions to Ask
- What's included in the venue fee?
- Is catering in-house or can we bring our own?
- What time can we access the venue for setup?
- When must the event end?
- What's your cancellation/postponement policy?
- Do you provide tables, chairs, linens?
- Is there an on-site coordinator?
- What are the bar/alcohol policies?
- Any noise restrictions or curfews?
- What's not allowed at the venue?

### Evaluation Criteria
- Overall atmosphere and aesthetics
- Location accessibility for guests
- Value for money
- Flexibility with vendors
- Staff professionalism
- Reviews and references

## General Vendor Evaluation Checklist

For ALL vendors, consider:

### Before Booking
- [ ] Checked online reviews (Google, Yelp, WeddingWire, The Knot)
- [ ] Asked for and contacted references
- [ ] Verified licensing and insurance
- [ ] Reviewed complete contract
- [ ] Understood payment schedule
- [ ] Confirmed availability for your date
- [ ] Discussed backup plans

### Red Flags to Watch For
- ❌ No written contract
- ❌ Requests full payment upfront
- ❌ No liability insurance
- ❌ Poor communication/responsiveness
- ❌ No portfolio or references
- ❌ Unclear pricing or hidden fees
- ❌ Unwilling to meet in person/video
- ❌ No cancellation/refund policy
- ❌ Pressure tactics to book immediately

### Contract Must-Haves
- [ ] Specific date and time
- [ ] Detailed services description
- [ ] Complete pricing breakdown
- [ ] Payment schedule
- [ ] Cancellation policy
- [ ] Contingency plans
- [ ] Overtime rates (if applicable)
- [ ] Deliverables and timeline
- [ ] Liability and insurance info
- [ ] Signatures from both parties

## Arizona-Specific Considerations

### Venue
- Heat management for outdoor October events
- Backup indoor space for unexpected weather
- Adequate shade structures
- Hydration stations for guests
- Sunset timing (earlier in October)

### Photography
- Experience with Arizona lighting conditions
- Knowledge of local scenic locations
- Heat-appropriate timing for outdoor photos

### Catering
- Food safety in warm weather
- Appropriate service style for climate
- Bar service in heat

### Flowers
- Hardy varieties for Arizona climate
- Availability of October blooms
- Proper hydration and refrigeration
