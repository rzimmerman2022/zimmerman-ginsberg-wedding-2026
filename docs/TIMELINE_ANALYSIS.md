## üí° PRO TIPS for November Wedding

1. **Weather:** November in most locations = cooler temps, possible rain
   - Have backup plan for outdoor elements
   - Consider heaters if outdoor reception
   - Indoor ceremony recommended

2. **Lighting:** Early sunset (4:30-5:30pm)
   - Plan ceremony by 3-4pm for natural light photos
   - OR do "First Look" before ceremony for portraits
   - Talk to photographer about timing

3. **Holiday Conflicts:**
   - Avoid weekend before Thanksgiving
   - Avoid Black Friday weekend (travel chaos)
   - Best: First 2-3 weekends of November

4. **Guest Travel:**
   - Send save-the-dates ASAP (6-8 months before)
   - Book hotel blocks early
   - November may have better pricing than peak summer
