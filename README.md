## Arizona Wedding Considerations

**October Weather:**
- Average highs: 85-90°F
- Average lows: 60-65°F
- Low humidity
- Minimal rain probability
- Excellent for outdoor ceremonies

**Popular Arizona Wedding Regions:**
- Phoenix/Scottsdale - Urban sophistication
- Sedona - Red rock scenery
- Tucson - Desert landscapes
- Flagstaff - Mountain/forest settings

---

## Notes

- October is peak wedding season in Arizona due to ideal weather
- Book venues 12-18 months in advance for October dates
- Consider indoor/outdoor combo for temperature flexibility
- Factor in out-of-state guest travel arrangements
